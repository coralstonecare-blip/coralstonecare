Historic Preservation

Gentle, meticulous restoration for delicate architectural elements, statues, and heritage facades. Our experts employ micro-abrasion and specialized poultices to remove decades of biological growth without degrading the original coral or limestone substrate.

Parks & Plazas

Heavy-duty restoration for large public areas facing intense environmental exposure and foot traffic. We implement deep-penetrating sealants to protect against urban pollutants, hard water staining, and biological degradation in open-air environments.

Why Specialized Care Matters

Standard pressure washing or generic commercial cleaners cause irreversible damage to porous stones like Coral and Oolitic Limestone, leading to pitting, crumbling, and accelerated organic growth. We use geologically appropriate methods.

  • check_circleLow-pressure steam & custom alkaline cleaners.
  • check_circleConsolidation treatments for decaying historic structures.
  • check_circleCommercial-grade, breathable penetrating sealers.
