Stone Restoration

Weathered, stained, and biologically degraded surfaces require a methodical approach. Our restoration protocol addresses deep-seated oxidation, organic growth, and micro-abrasions without compromising the structural integrity of porous limestone or coral stone.

Natural Stone Sealing

Porous materials demand impregnating sealers, not topical coatings. We utilize advanced fluorochemical technology that penetrates the substrate, forming a hydrophobic and oleophobic barrier while allowing vapor transmission.

  • shieldSub-surface invisible protection
  • water_dropMaintains natural stone breathability
  • blockPrevents efflorescence and salt spalling

Joint Sand Replacement

Failing joints are the primary cause of paver degradation. We extract exhausted material and install polymeric locking sand, creating a cohesive, flexible matrix that resists erosion and prevents weed germination.
